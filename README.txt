REQUIRMENTS:
- Solution Must Be Built In x86 architecture

FUNCTIONALITY:
- Point Lights
- Directional Lights
- SpotLights
- Rim Lighting
- Reflection
- Reflection Map
- Skybox
- CCW Face Culling
- Anti-Aliasing (MSAA x 4)
- Free Camera
- Sphere Meshes

CONTROLS:
W - Move Camera Forward
A - Move Camera Left
S - Move Camera Back
D - Move Camera Right
E - Move Camera WorldUp
Q - Move Camera WorldDown

TAB - Toggle Cursor (Starts Off)
CAPS - Toggle Camera Flashlight
ESC - Close Application

EXTRA FEATURES:
- Spotlight Capability
